Ariel

Due to confidentiality and security reasons, I cannot provide access to the project's code or disclose details. If you want to know more please contact me.

Description

Legal professionals often face lengthy hearings that can extend for hours or even days. In addition, they must tackle the reading and analysis of legal documents of considerable length and complexity. In this context, this application presents itself as a highly capable tool to assist in various legal tasks. Among its notable features are the ability to research laws and regulations, prepare and review legal documents, as well as efficiently organize and maintain legal files.

The platform offers users the opportunity to upload both audio files and text documents. Through a built-in chat within the ChatGPT API, interaction is enabled, allowing users to pose general questions related to laws and regulations. Furthermore, the option to make specific inquiries about documents previously uploaded to the platform is provided.

The system is divided into two key roles: that of the user and the administrator. Users have access to the functionality of engaging with the chat to ask general questions about legal matters and to make specific inquiries regarding audio files uploaded beforehand. On the other hand, administrators have the capability to manage user creation and deletion, as well as interact with the chat to pose detailed questions about text files previously uploaded to the platform.

In summary, this application provides a comprehensive solution for legal needs, enabling legal professionals and users, in general, to effectively and efficiently address a wide range of legal issues, from law research to interaction with specific documents, all backed by the power of artificial intelligence and ChatGPT's natural language processing capabilities.

Description

This platform has been developed to function as a video game exchange marketplace. Within this platform, different users have the opportunity to trade video games with one another. In the application, users can filter their search based on various criteria, such as their console preferences (be it Xbox, PlayStation, or both), as well as the game's condition, i.e., whether they wish to acquire new or used games. Furthermore, there is an option to specify the generation of the console to which the game belongs, allowing users to search for titles from either older or modern consoles.

Among the key functionalities of the application are the ability to register for those who do not have a user account and to log in if an account has already been created. Users can upload their video games onto the platform, along with relevant information about them. Additionally, a messaging feature is available, enabling users to communicate with others interested in exchanging video games.

Description

The purpose of this application is to assist guitarists in enhancing their musical ear through an evaluation of guitar chord identification. Within the platform, users can engage in training sessions designed to strengthen their ability to recognize guitar chords. Furthermore, they have the option to take a test in which they can customize the number of desired questions, as well as the variety and quantity of chord types they wish to identify.

During the test, a guitar chord will be randomly generated, and the goal is for the user to select the correct option corresponding to that chord. If the response is accurate, an animation will be displayed to confirm that the user chose the correct answer. In the event that the user chooses an incorrect answer, an animation will be provided to indicate the correct chord. Upon completion of the test, the user will be awarded a final score, assessing their performance in chord identification.

Description

This project has been developed using pure JavaScript (Vanilla JavaScript). The Intersection Observer functionality has been employed to carry out some of the animations on the site. Additionally, the BEM methodology (Block Element Modifier) has been implemented in the structuring and naming of CSS classes, contributing to greater code clarity and maintainability.

Regarding the styling aspect, Custom Properties in CSS have been leveraged, allowing flexibility in defining styles and facilitating their modification throughout the project.

A class-based approach has been applied in CSS to manage the animation logic, resulting in an effective organization of interface styles and behaviors.

Furthermore, a scroll control mechanism, both horizontal and vertical, has been implemented in the project, enabling smooth navigation for the user.
